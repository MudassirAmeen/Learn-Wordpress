Introduction :)

WordPress is a free and open-source content management system (CMS) based on PHP and MySQL. It was first released on May 27, 2003, by its founders Matt Mullenweg and Mike Little. Initially, WordPress was designed as a blogging platform, but over time it has evolved to become a powerful and flexible CMS that can be used to create a wide range of websites, from personal blogs to large corporate sites. It has a large community of users and developers who contribute to its development and support. Today, WordPress is the most popular website platform in the world, used by over 60 million websites.
